## The Problem

Most self-improvement apps are passive. They wait for you to show up.

For 18–24 year olds juggling school, work, and the chaos of early adulthood — that's not enough. You open the app once, forget about it for two weeks, and feel guilty. Generic reminders don't help. What you need is something that actually *shows up for you*.

**Arete flips the model. The app comes to you.**

---

## What Is Arete?

Arete is an AI-powered personal coaching app that builds a real picture of your habits and patterns — then proactively reaches out with specific, personalized coaching interventions rather than generic nudges.

No "drink more water" notifications. Real coaching, based on *your* data.

---

## How It Works

```
Onboard → Check In Daily → AI Learns Your Patterns → Proactive Coaching Reaches You
```

**🧠 Deep Onboarding**
A structured intake flow that builds a genuine model of your goals, habits, and lifestyle before day one.

**⏱️ 60-Second Daily Check-Ins**
Short enough to stick with. Rich enough to matter. Keeps your coaching fresh and data-driven every day.

**🍎 Apple Health Integration**
Sleep, activity, and biometric data feed directly into the coaching model. Interventions grounded in reality, not just self-report.

**🤖 AI Coaching Agent — Powered by Claude**
The core of Arete. An AI agent that proactively reaches out with personalized coaching based on your real patterns — not a chatbot you have to open.

**📱 Home Screen Widgets & Streaks**
Designed to live on your phone. Widgets, streak tracking, and smart push notifications make Arete feel alive in your daily life.
