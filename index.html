ἀρετή

ἀρετή  ·  ar·e·tḗ  ·  Ancient Greek

✦

"We are what we repeatedly do. Excellence, then, is not an act, but a habit."

Aristotle  ·  Nicomachean Ethics, 350 BC

The ancient Greeks believed ἀρετή was the highest calling of a human life. Not wealth. Not fame. The daily striving to become who you were capable of being. That idea is 2,400 years old and more relevant than anything you'll find in a self-help book. We named this app after it because we believe a daily practice, done with intention, with the right guide, changes everything.
